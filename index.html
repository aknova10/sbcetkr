EVENTS


INIZIO 5.0

The event was conducted for the freshers of 2019 on 17th August 2019 by IEEE SB CETkr. It started with an inaugural ceremony at 9.30 Am and ended by 10. 30 Am. MD session was conducted by NAZEEB ABDULLAH, a link representative. The session was informative and enthusiastic for the students and focused about the importance of being an IEEE member. Games and treasure hunt was given to students to keep up energy throughout the section.

REVIENS 2.0

The state level flagship event conducted by IEEE SB CETkr at College of Engineering Trikaripur. It was a 3 day program with wide range of inspirational and motivational speakers, technical workshops, cultural events, games and much more, and was well organised for the engineers to build up the future. The event was  scheduled with 3 different workshops in the most heeding facets of the globe. Ice breaking and delegates interactive sessions was held with the vision of networking within the delegates from different part of our state.







ABOUT US

IEEE Student Branch of College of Engineering Trikaripur was established in 2013. From the time of formation onwards, our student branch is actively organizing diverse events and participating in events all around the Kerala section.We have Power and Energy Society and Women in Engineering affinity group to empower the young women.

IEEE SB CETkr has members from all the departments.
