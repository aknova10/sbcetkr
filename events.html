COMPLETED EVENTS

INIZIO 5.0

The event conducted for the freshers of 2019 on 17th August 2019 by IEEE SB CETkr. It started with a inaugural ceremony at 9.30 Am and ended by 10. 30 Am. MD section was conducted which was led by NAZEEB ABDULLAH, link representative. The section was informative and enthusiastic for the students and focused about the importance of being an IEEE member. Games and treasure hunt was given to students to keep up energy throughout the section.

REVIENS 2.0

The state level student branch flagship event conducted by IEEE CETkr SB at college of engineering trikaripur. It was a 3 day program with wide range of inspirational and motivational speakers, technical workshops, cultural events, games and much more.which was  well organised for the engineers to build up the future. The event was  scheduled with 3 different workshops in the most heeding facets of the globe. Ice breaking and delegates interactive sessions was held along with the vision of networking within the delegates from different part of our state.

KREATE

A workshop on craft and dream catcher making was conducted by IEEE SB CETkr on 5th October 2019 in association with INQUA 3.0, National level tech fest of college of engineering trikaripur. The workshop was from 10.00 am to 3.00 pm. The dream catchers making was handled by Ms. Athira ravi and scrapbook making session handled by Ms. Arya PC.
